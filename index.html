WHAT IS CYBER AI

Cyber.ai. Is a crypto project that brings together the worlds of blockchain technology and artificialintelligence to create a decentralized economy. In other words, Cyber.ai is building a decentralized network composed of various autonomous agents.

These agents not only represent themselves, but also individuals, devices, and services. Moreover, Cyber.ai's mission is to create a permissionless system - meaning, autonomous agents will learn to evolve using artificial intelligence. The project leverages its native digital token, CAI , to facilitate autonomous economic activity and to reward agents for completing tasks.

ABOUT CAI

CAI TOKEN

The Cyber.AI token (“CAI”) is the native currency for the cyber.AI network and the keystone for a new, inclusive, borderless and permissionless digital economy. If Cyber.AI is the digital infrastructure, CAI token is the fuel that powers the network.

CAI is the currency required for the network and all the applications on top of it to function.CAI is designed as a payment method as well as a long-term reserve asset for the entire Cyber.AI economy. The token is used by validators, developers, and users to participate in the Cyber.AI network and earn rewards. It is also used for fees and to participate in future Artificial Intelligence governance.
